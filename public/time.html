Lash Care

  1. 1. Do not wet lashes within the first 24 hours.
  1. 2. Do not apply mascara or any oil based products on your lashes.
  1. 3. Do not curl apply any heat on your lashes.
  1. 4. Ensure that you clean your lashes daily.
  1. 5. Do not rub your lashes.
  1. 6. When washing your face, please do it in a gentle manner (you will be shown how to upon arrival).
